ABOUT

I am a filmmaker/animator/visual artist with a penchant for the ridiculous. With a passion for animation and film since a young age, I have grown to explore all types of motion-based media in pursuit of my bizarre ideas. Sometimes, I try to scare. Other times, confuse. Usually, I just try to make you laugh.

I am currently developing projects for various internet-based aliases, all while pursuing a B.F.A in Film and Television at the Tisch School of the Arts, New York University.
